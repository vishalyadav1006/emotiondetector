Emotions

Emotion is a complex psychological state that involves three key components: a subjective experience, a physiological response, and an expressive behavior. It's how we react to various stimuli or situations and can range from feelings like happiness, sadness, and anger to more nuanced emotions like nostalgia or anxiety. Emotions play a crucial role in our decision-making, social interactions, and overall well-being. They help us connect with others and navigate our experiences in the world.
